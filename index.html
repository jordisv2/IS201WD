About This Blog

My intent isn’t to use this blog to vent about the bad dates that I go on. What I want to do is start an honest conversation with other people who want to talk about their experiences in dating as well. Maybe even shift some perspectives on what this world is and how we view it. Like I said, we all talk about how bad things are in our experience, but I want to take it further and create solutions to these frustrations. I hope you’re curious because I am too. I’m ready to start talking and start exploring.
